# Contributing to Redrill

Redrill is a self-hosted daemon that proves backups are restorable by actually
restoring them. It's primarily built and maintained by one person and is first
and foremost something to **use** — so the most valuable thing you can do is tell
me what's broken or missing.

## Open an issue

Bug reports and ideas are welcome through issues — especially **which backup
tools you'd want supported next**. Helpful details: what you ran and expected,
what actually happened (logs help — Redrill redacts secrets, but double-check
before pasting), and your environment (Redrill version, OS, backup engine +
version).
